Specials

Throughout the school year, students participate in consecutive 22-day sessions of each of the following eight Specials: Conflict Resolution/Transition, Art, Physical Education, Technology Education, Computer Literacy, Music, Health, and Family & Consumer Science. In addition, students (except for Music) participate in an extra Specials lab one day per week.

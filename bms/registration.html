New Student Registration

Below you will find the forms required to register your child at Bow Memorial School. In order for a registration to be processed, all documents must be completed and turned into the BMS office. You may bring in or mail your documents to:

Bow Memorial School
20 Bow Center Road
Bow, NH 03304

Registration Checklist

  • Completed registration form

  • Copy of birth certificate

  • Completed and signed residency declaration form

  • Proof of residency. Requires two of the following documents:

    • utility bill indicating legal residency
    • lease agreement or rent receipt indicating legal residence with the landlord’s address and telephone number
    • documentation of home ownership in the town of Bow.

  • Copies of all legal documents/court orders (parenting plan and custody guidelines, guardianship, restraining orders, orders with regard to educational records)

Chorus

The fall season provided the BMS choruses with several opportunities to perform. The choruses performed the “National Anthem” at the Open Houses in September. Fifth and sixth grade choruses presented fall performances at Presidential Oaks retirement community on October 24 and at Bow Elementary School on October 26. The seventh and eighth grade chorus will be performing at White Rock Senior Living Community on November 19.

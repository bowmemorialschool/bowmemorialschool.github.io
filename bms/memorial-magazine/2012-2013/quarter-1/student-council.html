Student Council

November 8, 2012

Student Council is off to a terrific start! The council has elected this year’s executive board, and they are: President — Olivia Krause, Vice-President — Sam Grondin, Recording Secretary — Shannon Murray, Corresponding Secretary — Sara Anoli, and Treasurer — Gwen Molind. Congratulations, one and all!

Council held the first dance on October 12. This fall-themed dance was well attended and students had a great time! Thank you to all our wonderful parent chaperones who make our dances possible!

Council has kept busy organizing a variety of events. They planned Red Ribbon Week activities in conjunction with the guidance and nursing staff. Many council members, chorus students, and a few additional students volunteered to go to Presidential Oaks on Wednesday, October 24, for the annual after-school field trip. Once there, all students visited with the nursing home residents and handed out flowers and homemade cards. The BMS chorus sang fall and Halloween-related songs. What a wonderful community service event!

On Friday, October 26, the council hosted the Annual BMS Autumn Fest assembly in the gym from 2:00-3:00 p.m. It was a lively school-spirited assembly at which we celebrated our Red Ribbon Week pledge numbers and grade-level classroom winners of the Dress Red competition. We also recognized and celebrated the Bow Reads program, the Bow PTO Magazine Drive, and BMS fall sports teams. Finally, we enjoyed a few audience participation games!

November is right around the corner, and council will soon be shopping for the annual Thanksgiving basket items to donate to Bow Welfare. Also coming in November is the Bow PTO Craft Fair at BHS on November 17. Some of our council members will volunteer to assist crafters set up their booths during the afternoon of November 16.

Thank You!

Jan Eskeland and Terri George
BMS Student Council Co-Advisors

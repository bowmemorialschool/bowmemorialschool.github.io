REACH

June 13, 2013
  • Visiting an operating room
  • Stock market performance
  • BMS Expressions

The BMS Mentoring Program had another successful year. Rob Kramer’s sixth grade science group had the rare opportunity to visit an operating room and learn about the anesthesia equipment used during a surgery. Derek Wong mentored a 7th and 8th grade stock market group and conclu ded the year looking at how each student's stocks performed over the year. I would like to extend a huge thank you to all our mentors for their time, hard work, and dedication this year.

The BMS Expressions, our literary magazine, will be available to all students and faculty this month. Thank you to all the student contributors; without them, there would be no magazine.

If you would like more information on any REACH programs, please contact Christy Romano at cromano@bownet.org or call #225-3212 ext. 416.

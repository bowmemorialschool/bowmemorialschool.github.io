Best Buddies Walk and 5K

On June 1, the Best Buddies Friendship Walk and 5K Run took place in Concord at the State House Plaza. The goal of the walk was to promote inclusion, friendships, and opportunities for individuals with and without intellectual and developmental disabilities. This year a group of students, families, and staff representing Bow Memorial School took part and raised a total of $1,590 to support Best Buddies NH. Special thanks to Jack Rich, an 8th grader at BMS, who was the 3rd highest fundraiser in the state!

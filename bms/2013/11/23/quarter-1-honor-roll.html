Quarter 1 Honor Roll

November 23, 2013

19 eighth graders and 29 seventh graders earned High Honors for the quarter, and 24 eighth graders and 46 seventh graders earned Honors. Congratulations to these students on their accomplishments!

To earn High Honors, a student must earn a grade of 90% or above in each subject area including Specials, Band, and/or Chorus; for Honors, the grade must be 83% or above.

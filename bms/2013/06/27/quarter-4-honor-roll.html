Quarter 4 Honor Roll

June 27, 2013

29 eighth graders and 33 seventh graders earned High Honors for the quarter, and 29 eighth graders and 40 seventh graders earned Honors. Congratulations to these students on their accomplishments!

To earn High Honors, a student must earn a grade of 90% or above in each subject area including Specials, Band, and/or Chorus; for Honors, the grade must be 83% or above.

Quarter 1 Honor Roll

December 21, 2012

26 eighth graders and 43 seventh graders earned High Honors for the quarter, and 49 eighth graders and 49 seventh graders earned Honors. Congratulations to these students on their accomplishments!

To earn High Honors, a student must earn a grade of 90% or above in each subject area including Specials, Band, and/or Chorus; for Honors, the grade must be 83% or above.

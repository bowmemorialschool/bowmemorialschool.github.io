Grade 8

June 13, 2013
  • Building catamarans
  • Studying aquatic biology
  • High ropes course
  • Working in a pioneer village
  • Karaoke Night

The 8th grade spent a fabulous week out at Sargent Center in Hancock, NH from May 20-24. We were treated to wonderful food, cooperative weather (for the most part), awesome instructors, and a chance to get to know fellow students. The students built catamarans, studied aquatic biology, visited and worked an authentic New England pioneer village, tested their resolve on the high ropes course and dangle quad, and sang their hearts out at Karaoke Night and the campfire. This year marked the 25th year that Bow Memorial School has attended Sargent Center. We are looking forward to continuing this tradition for many years to come. Take a look at some of our students in action!

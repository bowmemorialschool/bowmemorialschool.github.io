BAGGO Champion Emerges!

On Monday, June 10, the fifth graders all gathered on the baseball field to compete in the Annual BAGGO Tournament. With their evenly-spaced boards lining the outfield, students began tossing their corn-filled bags back and forth, hoping to be the first to reach 21 points. Some of their handsewn bags landed on the ground, some on the board for a point, while others flopped their way right through the corn hole for three points! As specials teachers worked their way through their single-elimination brackets, the field of competitors thinned. At the end of 8 rounds, four finalists emerged: Meri Krause would face off against Riley Elliot for the championship, and Taylor Burnham would play opposite Eric Hodgkins for third place honors. As their enthusiastic classmates chanted and cheered them on, the players perfected their tosses. In the end, iTunes gift cards were awarded to Riley, Baggo Champion; Meri, runner-up; and Taylor, our third-place winner. What a great way to culminate our specials integrated project!

In July
by Evaleen Stein

Let us find a shady wady
Pretty little brook;
Let us have some candy handy,
And a picture book.
There all day we'll stay and play and
Never mind the heat,
While the water gleaming, streaming,
Ripples round our feet.
And we'll gather curly pearly
Mussel shells while bright
Frightened minnows darting, parting,
Scurry out of sight.
What if, what if, —heigho! my oh!—
All the "ifs" were true,
And the little fishes wishes,
Now, what would you do?

Poem submitted by Deborah Liebson, Health

Art Concord Youth Show

Several Bow Elementary and Bow Memorial students submitted their artwork to the Art Concord Youth Show, which was held in April at 38 Main Street. Twenty students from area schools were chosen for the People's Choice Award and had their work displayed at the Kimball Jenkins Estate.

Congratulations to Henry Fleury for receiving the People's Choice Award!

The works of Lauryn Hrinchuk, Lauren Roy, Elizabeth Hunter, James Jensen, and Spencer DesFosses were also displayed.
